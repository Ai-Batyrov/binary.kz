Delivery and Payment

Our online store cooperates with several delivery services. Our partners will deliver to any point of Kazakhstan, safe and sound.
Kazpost service accepts orders in the time interval: 08:00 - 22:00. There are no weekends. Delivers the goods within a week.

Exline service accepts orders in the around the clock. Weekend on Sundays. Delivers the goods within a week or through fast-delivery within 3-4 days.

Post Express service accepts orders in the around the clock. Weekend on Saturdays and Sundays. Delivers the goods within a week or through fast-delivery within 3-4 days.

Avis service accepts orders in the around the clock. Delivers the goods within a day, since the service works only in the city of Almaty.
In our store you can pay or arrange installments through banks: Kaspi Bank, Halyk Bank, Jusan Bank
